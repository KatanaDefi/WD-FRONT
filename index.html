FAQ

WHAT IS WESTIE DOGE?
WESTIE DOGE $WESTY IS A COMMUNITY TOKEN THAT REWARDS ITS HOLDERS WITH $CAKE. HOLD WESTIE DOGE $WESTY YOU WILL RECEIVE 4% OF EVERY TRANSACTION IN $CAKE AS REWARD.
HOW TO PARTICIPATE TO WESTIE DOGE PRIVATE SALE?
IN ORDER TO JOIN THE PRIVATE SALE INVESTORS MUST JOIN THE WHITELIST.
WHEN WILL THE $WESTY TOKEN BE LAUNCHED?
$WESTY TOKEN WILL BE LAUNCHED AFTER THE PRESALE AT PINKSALE.FINANCE. 60% OF THE LIQUDITY WILL BE LOCKED & THE REMAINING SUPPLY WILL BE ALSO LOCKED IN SMART CONTRACT
WHERE TO BUY $WESTY TOKEN AND WHAT SLIPPAGE NEEDS TO BE USED?
AFTER LAUNCH, YOU WILL BE ABLE TO BUY $WESTY TOKEN ON PANCAKESWAP AND POOCOIN. WE RECOMMAND TO SET THE SLIPPAGE TO 14% OR 16%.
